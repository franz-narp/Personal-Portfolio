Our Services

Affordable Healthcare for Every Filipino

At The Generics Pharmacy (TGP), we believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to all. Our commitment is to make safe, effective, and affordable generic medicines available to every Filipino, wherever they are in the country.

With over 2,000 branches nationwide, TGP continues to serve as a trusted partner in health and wellness, providing a wide range of services that bring care closer to communities.

What We Offer

  • 💊 Affordable Generic Medicines
    We provide a wide selection of FDA-approved generic medicines, ensuring that every Filipino has access to high-quality treatments without the high price.
  • ✓ Over-the-Counter (OTC) Products
    From vitamins and supplements to basic first-aid needs, our OTC products support your family’s daily health and well-being.
  • 🩺 Pharmacy Assistance and Consultation
    Our friendly and knowledgeable pharmacists are always ready to guide customers in choosing the right medicine, giving proper dosage advice, and ensuring safe usage.
  • ❣️ Community Health Support
    TGP partners with local communities and organizations to promote health awareness, medical missions, and affordable healthcare programs that reach underserved areas.
  • 🌿 Health and Wellness Products
    Beyond medicines, we also offer wellness essentials, including herbal and personal care products, for a more holistic approach to good health.
